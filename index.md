## 🔍 OCR Tips
Optical Character Recognition turns scanned pages into text. To get the best results:

* Use high-resolution scans for better accuracy.
* Ensure the orientation of the document is straight.
* Clean your scanner glass to avoid streaks on the digital image.
* Select the correct language in the OCR settings before you start the conversion.
